About Us

Mission and Vision:

Our mission is to help people pick the right stocks listed on S&P 500. This webiste is for people who has no knowledge or very little knowledge of investments in stock trading and stock analysis.

Our goal is to make investor's decision more reliable, effortless and time saving. Moreover, this product allows user to take calculated risk instead of guessing, and avoids investors bias including cognitive bias and emotional bias.





Our Story:

This website was started as a capstone project for masters degree program at Northwestern Polytechnic University. This website is developed by the team who has background in economics, finance and IT.

We started this project as in order to apply our of business and IT in order to create a stock recommendation system.
